What We Accept

We specialize in recycling Macs, iPads, iPhones, and other electronics to reduce e-waste and give back to the community.

  • Free pickup for businesses and individuals
  • Safe and secure recycling of Macs, iPads, and iPhones
  • Refurbished tech for those in need

Why Recycle?

Protect the Environment: Avoid harmful e-waste in landfills.

Data Security: We ensure secure wiping or destruction of sensitive data.

Support the Community: Recycled tech can be refurbished for those in need.
