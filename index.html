What We Do

At MN Computer Recycling, we specialize in recycling Macs and other gently used electronics to reduce e-waste and give back to the community.

  • Free pickup for businesses and individuals
  • Safe and secure recycling of Macs, iPads, and iPhones
  • Refurbished tech for those in need

Why Choose Us?

By recycling your old technology with us, you’re supporting Minnesota’s local community and environment:

  • Reduce e-waste: Prevent harmful electronics from ending up in landfills.
  • Community impact: Gently used devices are refurbished and donated to people in need.
  • Local focus: We’re dedicated to serving businesses and individuals in Minnesota.
