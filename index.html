What We Accept

We specialize in recycling Macs, iPads, iPhones, high-end speakers, and receivers. We also accept all brands of computers and accessories like keyboards, mice, and cables.

Why Recycle?

Protect the Environment: Avoid harmful e-waste in landfills.

Data Security: We ensure secure wiping or destruction of sensitive data.

Support the Community: Recycled tech can be refurbished for those in need.
